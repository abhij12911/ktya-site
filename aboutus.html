Introduction: Krishna Township Youth Association (KTYA) is a community-based organization that has been serving the residents of Krishna Township for over 27 years. We are dedicated to preserving and promoting the cultural heritage of our community through the celebration of traditional festivals like Ganesh Chaturthi and Navratra, as well as through various social and community service activities.

About Us: KTYA was formed in 1994 by a group of young residents of Krishna Township who wanted to bring the community together and preserve the cultural traditions of their ancestors. Over the years, our organization has grown and evolved, but our mission has remained the same - to promote and preserve the cultural heritage of our community through the celebration of traditional festivals and through various social and community service activities.
Events & Festivals we organise : Ganesh Chaturthi is one of the most important festivals in the Hindu calendar and is celebrated with great enthusiasm and devotion in our community. KTYA organizes a grand celebration of this festival every year, complete with traditional prayers, music, and dance performances, and a grand procession of Lord Ganesha's idol.
Navratra is another important festival that we celebrate with great enthusiasm and devotion. Our Navratra celebrations include traditional prayers, music, and dance performances, as well as a grand aarti and a grand procession of the Navdurga idols.
Community Services:
In addition to organizing festivals, KTYA also plays an active role in the community by organizing various social and community service activities throughout the year. Some of our activities include:
Blood donation camps: We regularly organize blood donation camps in partnership with local hospitals and blood banks to help meet the blood needs of the community.
Book distribution: We distribute books to underprivileged children in the community to promote education and literacy.
Society neat and clean competition:We organize competitions to encourage residents of the community to keep their surroundings clean and maintain a healthy environment.
Tree plantation:We organize tree plantation drives to promote environmental conservation and create a greener community.
